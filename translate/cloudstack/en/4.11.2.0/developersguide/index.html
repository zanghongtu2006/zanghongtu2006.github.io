开发者手册¶

This is the Apache CloudStack 开发者手册. This section gives information for those wishing to develop CloudStack either contributing to the CloudStack core software or writing external plugins. Futher information can also be found at CloudStack’s wiki https://cwiki.apache.org/confluence/display/CLOUDSTACK/Home and on the CloudStack mailing lists http://cloudstack.apache.org/mailing-lists.html
